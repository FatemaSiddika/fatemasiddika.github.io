Welcome!

I am a Graduate student at Department of Computer Science in Iowa State University. I am also working as a Graduate Teaching Assistant.

Research Interest

My research interest falls mainly into Machine Learning, Internet of Things, Sensor and Ad Hoc Networks, Distributed Systems, Edge Computing. Findings of my undergraduate thesis with Sajeeb Saha is published in the International Conference on Networking, Systems and Security (NSysS), 2017 (IEEE). My masters thesis work is under the supervision of Dr. Uzzal Kumar Acharjee based on Semantic Segmentation using Deep Learning. I have worked as an undergraduate research fellow at Information Technology Research and Resource Center (ITRRC), Jagananth University.

I am always open to interesting discussions and collaborations. Biography »
